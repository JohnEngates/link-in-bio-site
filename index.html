About

John Engates builds AI agent systems that coordinate multiple autonomous agents to solve problems single agents can't. Over 30 years he's co-founded one of Texas's first ISPs, built hosting infrastructure for early YouTube, and created the distributed storage system that would become OpenStack Swift.

At Rackspace (CTO, 2000-2018), his team built and managed the infrastructure that kept YouTube running as it grew from startup to billion-user platform acquired by Google. His team scaled "Fanatical Support," 24/7 expert help that became the company's identity. The Intensive Hosting division he ran supported some of the web's most demanding workloads when "the cloud" meant actual servers in actual data centers.

In 2009, when a Facebook hardware engineering leader asked about open-sourcing data center hardware, John committed Rackspace as a founding member of the Open Compute Project. That same year, his team built the distributed storage system that would become OpenStack Swift. It now runs at CERN, Wikimedia, and hundreds of deployments worldwide.

The White House called in 2013 when Healthcare.gov's launch crisis needed infrastructure expertise. He sat in the Situation Room with heads of HHS and CMS, explaining what went wrong. At Cloudflare (Field CTO, 2021-2025), he guided enterprise AI and security deployments. Today he serves on the boards of Frost Bank (NYSE: CFR) and James Avery Artisan Jewelry, mentors Techstars companies, and leads SIM San Antonio.

Speaker Bio

John Engates builds multi-agent AI systems that coordinate swarms of autonomous agents to tackle problems too complex for single agents. He focuses on practical enterprise deployment: security incident response, infrastructure management, workflow orchestration.

Over 30 years he's co-founded one of Texas's first ISPs and served as Rackspace CTO for 18 years. At Rackspace, he built infrastructure for YouTube's growth and co-created OpenStack Swift. He's been called to the White House to advise on Healthcare.gov's 2013 crisis and led Cloudflare's Field CTO team helping enterprises deploy AI and zero-trust architectures.

His current focus is agent systems that genuinely work in production, not demos. He serves on the boards of Frost Bank (NYSE: CFR) and James Avery Artisan Jewelry, mentors Techstars companies, and speaks on practical AI deployment for enterprises.
